GST Registration

Any business with an annual turnover exceeding the threshold limit must register for GST. We make this process simple and fast, ensuring you receive your Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN) without any complications. Our experts handle document verification and application filing, keeping you informed at every step.

  • For businesses with turnover > â‚¹40 Lakhs (Goods) or â‚¹20 Lakhs (Services).
  • Mandatory for e-commerce sellers and interstate suppliers.
  • Receive your GSTIN in as little as 3-7 working days.
Start Your Registration

GST Return Filing

Stay compliant by filing your GST returns on time. We handle the preparation and filing of your monthly (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B), quarterly (QRMP), and other applicable returns. Our meticulous process ensures accuracy in ITC claims and tax calculations, helping you avoid penalties and interest.

  • Accurate filing of GSTR-1 (Outward Supplies) & GSTR-3B (Summary Return).
  • Proper reconciliation with GSTR-2A/2B to maximize Input Tax Credit.
  • Timely reminders and filing to avoid late fees.
File Your GST Return

GST Annual Return (GSTR-9)

GSTR-9 is an annual return to be filed by all registered taxpayers. It's a consolidation of all your monthly/quarterly returns. For larger businesses, GSTR-9C (a reconciliation statement) is also required. We provide expert assistance in preparing and filing these complex annual returns accurately.

  • Mandatory for taxpayers with turnover above â‚¹2 Crore.
  • Detailed reconciliation of your entire year's financial data.
  • Preparation and certification of GSTR-9C by a professional.
File GSTR-9 / 9C

GST e-Invoicing

e-Invoicing is mandatory for businesses with a specified turnover, where B2B invoices are authenticated electronically by GSTN for further use on the common GST portal. We help you integrate your billing system with the Invoice Registration Portal (IRP) and ensure a smooth, compliant e-invoicing process.

  • Currently applicable for businesses with turnover > â‚¹5 Crore.
  • Real-time generation of Invoice Reference Number (IRN) and QR codes.
  • Automates tax return filing and e-way bill generation.
Implement e-Invoicing

GST Cancellation

If you are closing your business or if your turnover has fallen below the threshold limit, you may need to cancel your GST registration. We provide a hassle-free service to file the final returns and submit the cancellation application (Form GST REG-16), ensuring a clean exit without future liabilities.

  • Filing of final return in Form GSTR-10.
  • Proper clearance of all tax liabilities.
  • Guidance on the complete legal procedure for closure.
Cancel GST Registration

GST Notice Reply

Receiving a notice from the GST department can be stressful. Our team of tax experts will help you understand the notice, prepare a detailed and factual reply, and represent you before the authorities if needed. We handle notices related to discrepancies in returns, ITC claims, audits, and more.

  • Analysis of the notice and its implications.
  • Drafting and filing of a legally sound reply.
  • Liaison with the GST department on your behalf.
