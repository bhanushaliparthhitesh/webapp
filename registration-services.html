MSME / Udyam Registration

Registering your business as a Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprise (MSME) through the Udyam portal unlocks numerous government benefits. This includes access to collateral-free bank loans, subsidies on patents and trademarks, protection against delayed payments, and eligibility for various government schemes and tenders.

  • Access to government subsidies & benefits.
  • Eligibility for priority sector lending from banks.
  • Receive your Udyam certificate in 1-2 working days.
Register as MSME

Startup India Registration

The Startup India initiative provides a platform for innovative startups to grow. By getting recognized under this scheme, your business can avail a 3-year tax holiday, access to a Fund of Funds for capital, simplified compliance, and opportunities to network and participate in government projects.

  • Enjoy a 3-year income tax holiday on profits.
  • Access to government funds and venture capital networks.
  • Fast-tracked patent applications and IPR protection.
Get Startup Recognition

FSSAI Registration

A Food Safety and Standard Authority of India (FSSAI) license is mandatory for any business involved in the manufacturing, processing, storage, distribution, or sale of food products. It ensures food quality and safety, building consumer trust and legal compliance. We help you get the right FSSAI license—Basic, State, or Central—based on your business scale.

  • Mandatory for all food business operators.
  • Builds credibility and customer trust.
  • Ensures legal compliance and avoids penalties.
Get FSSAI License

Import Export Code (IEC)

The Import Export Code (IEC) is a mandatory 10-digit code for any business engaged in the import or export of goods and services from India. Issued by the DGFT, this code has lifetime validity and is essential for clearing customs, shipping goods, and processing bank transactions for foreign trade.

  • Enables global market access for your products.
  • Lifetime validity with no need for renewal.
  • Quick online application process.
Get Your IEC

PF Registration

Provident Fund (PF) registration is mandatory for businesses employing 20 or more people. It is a social security scheme that provides retirement benefits to employees. Compliance with EPF regulations is crucial for employee welfare and avoiding legal penalties. We handle the complete registration and guide you on monthly compliance.

  • Ensures compliance with employee welfare laws.
  • Provides a retirement fund for your employees.
  • Hassle-free online registration process.
Register for PF

ESI Registration

Employee State Insurance (ESI) is a health insurance scheme for Indian workers. It is mandatory for establishments with 10 or more employees (20 in some states). ESI provides medical, sickness, maternity, and disability benefits to employees and their families, ensuring their well-being and security.

  • Comprehensive health insurance coverage for employees.
  • Fulfills legal requirements for employee benefits.
  • Access to a wide network of ESI hospitals and dispensaries.
Register for ESI
