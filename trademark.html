Trademark Registration

Before filing, a thorough trademark search is crucial to ensure your desired brand name or logo is not already in use. We conduct a comprehensive search across the trademark database and then file your application under the correct class, maximizing your chances of a smooth registration.

  • Detailed search report to assess availability.
  • Classification of goods and services under 45 classes.
  • Use of the ™ symbol immediately after filing.
Start Your Trademark Search

Trademark Objection

Sometimes, the Trademark Registry raises objections on an application based on absolute or relative grounds. A strong, well-drafted reply with supporting documents and case laws is required to overcome these objections. Our experts handle this process to get your application back on track.

  • Analysis of the examination report and grounds for objection.
  • Drafting a legal and factual reply within the 30-day deadline.
  • Submission of evidence of use to strengthen your claim.
File Objection Reply

Trademark Opposition

After your trademark application is published in the journal, any third party can oppose it within four months. We help you defend your mark by filing a counter-statement and evidence. We can also help you oppose a published mark that is confusingly similar to yours.

  • Filing/defending notice of opposition.
  • Drafting counter-statements and evidence.
  • Strategic advice on opposition proceedings.
Handle Opposition

Trademark Hearing

If the examiner is not satisfied with the written reply to an objection, a hearing is scheduled. This is an opportunity to present your case orally before the Registrar of Trademarks. Our experienced trademark attorneys will represent you, arguing effectively to get your mark accepted for registration.

  • Preparation of arguments and case materials.
  • Representation by qualified trademark lawyers.
  • Handling all procedural formalities for the hearing.
Arrange for Hearing

Trademark Renewal

A registered trademark is valid for 10 years from the date of application. It must be renewed to keep it in force. We manage the renewal process for you, filing the application before the deadline to ensure your brand protection continues uninterrupted for another decade.

  • Protection for another 10-year period.
  • Filing can be done within one year before expiry.
  • Option to restore the trademark even after expiry.
Renew Your Trademark

Trademark Transfer (Assignment)

Trademark assignment is the process of transferring ownership of a trademark from one person or entity to another. This can be done with or without the goodwill of the business. We assist in drafting the assignment agreement and filing the necessary forms with the registry to record the change in ownership.

  • Transfer ownership of your brand legally.
  • Drafting of a legally sound Trademark Assignment Agreement.
  • Filing of Form TM-P to record the assignment.
Assign Your Trademark
