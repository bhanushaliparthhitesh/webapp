PAN Card Services

The Permanent Account Number (PAN) is a unique ten-digit alphanumeric number issued by the Income Tax Department. It is mandatory for all financial transactions. We assist in applying for a new PAN card and making corrections to existing ones.

  • Application for new PAN for individuals and businesses.
  • Correction of details like name, date of birth, etc.
  • Track the status of your PAN card application.
Apply for PAN

Income Tax Return Filing

Filing your Income Tax Return (ITR) is a legal obligation. We simplify this process for salaried individuals, freelancers, and businesses. Our experts ensure all your income sources, deductions, and exemptions are correctly reported to maximize your refund and ensure compliance.

  • For Salaried, Freelancers, and Business Owners.
  • Claim all eligible deductions under Section 80C, 80D, etc.
  • Accurate computation of capital gains and other incomes.
File Your ITR

TDS Return Filing

If you are a business owner, you are required to deduct tax at source (TDS) on certain payments and file TDS returns. We manage the entire TDS compliance process, from calculating TDS and depositing it on time to filing quarterly returns (Form 24Q for salaries, Form 26Q for other payments) and issuing TDS certificates.

  • Timely filing of quarterly TDS returns.
  • Accurate calculation and deposit of TDS.
  • Generation and issuance of Form 16/16A to deductees.
File TDS Returns

TAN Registration

Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number (TAN) is a 10-digit alphanumeric number required for all persons who are responsible for deducting or collecting tax. We assist you in obtaining your TAN registration quickly and efficiently, so you can comply with TDS regulations.

  • Mandatory for all tax deductors.
  • Hassle-free application process.
  • Get your TAN within 7-10 working days.
Get Your TAN

15CA - 15CB Filing

When making payments to a non-resident, a declaration in Form 15CA and a certificate from a Chartered Accountant in Form 15CB are required. We provide expert services for the preparation and filing of these forms to ensure your foreign remittances are compliant with the Income Tax Act.

  • For payments made to non-residents.
  • Certification by a qualified Chartered Accountant.
  • Ensures compliance with international tax regulations.
File 15CA-15CB

Income Tax Notice Reply

Receiving a notice from the Income Tax Department can be daunting. Our team of experts helps you understand the reason for the notice, gathers the necessary information, and drafts a comprehensive and accurate response. We ensure your reply is filed within the due date to avoid further complications.

  • Expert analysis of tax notices (e.g., u/s 143(1), 139(9), 148).
  • Preparation and submission of a legally sound reply.
  • Representation before tax authorities if required.
