Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about our services, processes, and how we can help your business grow.

Business registration typically takes 7-15 working days, depending on the type of business structure you choose. For Private Limited Companies, it takes about 10-15 days, while LLP registration can be completed in 7-10 days. We handle all the paperwork and government procedures on your behalf, so you can focus on your business.

For GST registration, you'll need:

  • PAN card and Aadhaar card of the business owner
  • Business address proof (rent agreement or ownership documents)
  • Bank account details and cancelled cheque
  • Digital signature certificate (for companies)

No, it's not mandatory to file income tax returns if your total income is below the basic exemption limit (₹2.5 lakhs for FY 2023-24). However, filing returns is recommended even with zero income as it helps establish your tax compliance history and may be required for various purposes like loan applications, visa processing, or future business ventures.

Late GST filing attracts the following penalties:

  • Late fee: ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) up to a maximum of ₹10,000
  • Interest: 18% per annum on the tax amount due
  • Additional consequences: Input tax credit may be denied for delayed filings

While you can technically file a trademark application yourself, it's highly recommended to use professional services like Big4India. Our experts help you conduct thorough searches to avoid conflicts, prepare proper documentation, handle objections, and ensure your application has the best chance of approval. The trademark registration process can take 18-24 months, and professional guidance can save you time and money in the long run.

MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) compliance involves filing various returns and maintaining statutory records for companies and LLPs. It's crucial because:

  • Legal requirement: Non-compliance can lead to penalties and even company strike-off
  • Business credibility: Proper compliance enhances your business reputation
  • Funding opportunities: Investors and lenders prefer compliant companies

Tax savings vary based on your income level and business structure. With proper tax planning, individuals can save 15-30% of their tax liability through legitimate deductions and exemptions. For businesses, savings can be even higher (20-40%) through strategic planning of expenses, depreciation, and business structure optimization. Our experts analyze your specific situation to maximize savings while ensuring full compliance with tax laws.
