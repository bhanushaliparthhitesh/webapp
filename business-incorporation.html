Private Limited Company

A Private Limited Company is a privately-held business entity. It offers limited liability to its owners (shareholders) and has a separate legal existence from them. It's the most popular corporate structure for new businesses in India.

  • Limited liability protection for owners.
  • Separate legal entity status.
  • Easier to raise funds and attract investors.
Register a Pvt. Ltd.

One Person Company (OPC)

An OPC is a type of company that can be formed with just one member. It's a perfect choice for solo entrepreneurs who want to enjoy the benefits of a corporate structure, such as limited liability, without the need for multiple partners.

  • Single owner with full control.
  • Limited liability and separate legal entity.
  • Less compliance compared to a Private Limited Company.
Register an OPC

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

An LLP combines the advantages of both a partnership and a company into a single form of organization. Partners have limited liability, and the LLP has a separate legal identity. It's a popular choice for professionals like lawyers, accountants, and consultants.

  • Limited liability for all partners.
  • Flexible internal structure and management.
  • Lower compliance costs than a company.
Register an LLP

Partnership Firm

A Partnership Firm is a business structure where two or more individuals manage and operate a business in accordance with the terms and objectives set out in a Partnership Deed. It's relatively easy to start and has minimal compliance requirements.

  • Easy to form and manage.
  • Minimal compliance and regulations.
  • Shared decision-making and responsibilities.
Register a Partnership

Sole Proprietorship

A Sole Proprietorship is the simplest form of business, where the business is owned and managed by a single person. There is no legal distinction between the owner and the business. It's easy to set up and gives the owner complete control.

  • Easiest and quickest to set up.
  • Full control and decision-making power.
  • Minimal legal formalities and compliance.
